"So, who is this guy?"

    This is a guy who just hand-coded this page without the help of any measly "WYSIWYG" editors. This is a guy who had no prior experience with HTML or CSS but diligently embraced this challenge, having only 10 days to learn the basics before presenting what you see before you today.

    This is what I live for; accelerated learning and the pursuit of knowledge. Education, a cornerstone in my life, has provided me endless opportunities to think outside the box and to demonstrate my leadership as part of a team.

    Although being able to learn code from the couch is cool, the ability to work as part of a team solving real world problems in an efficient, logical, and immersive environment is truly priceless. I am prepared to rigorously continue my self-education and strengthen my foundations so that I may be of the most benefit to my cohort, when you select me for the May '14 Launch Academy program.




 "A Man with a Plan!"

     This three phase plan can't be beat! To thrive in a team learning environment such as Launch Academy, you must come to the table equipped with a strong knowledge of the core materials. This helps everybody!

  • Phase 1: Complete first coding challenge and deliver a hand-coded website to demonstrate ability to learn under pressure while still paying attention to detail.
  • Phase 2: Continue learning as much code as possible by capitalizing on the tremendous amount of free resources available online. As well as learning more HTML and CSS, I plan to learn jQuery and finally finish my copy of "Learn Python the Hard Way."
  • Phase 3: This is where Launch Academy comes in to merge my self-education with team-based, real world problem solving challenges! This is where all the magic happens and the fun just begins!! After graduating from Launch Academy I will have all the skills necessary to make an impact!!!
